Chicken Spicy Macaroni

My Special Spicy Chicken Macaroni Recipe

This recipe means a lot to me because my mom has been making it for me and my siblings since 2017. From the very first time I tried it, I completely fell in love with it. It’s spicy, full of flavor, and the kind of dish that instantly makes your mouth water — especially if you’re a fan of bold, spicy food!

I learned this recipe from my mom, and now it’s become my own special version. I wanted to share it with everyone who loves macaroni or spaghetti but prefers something with a little more heat. Most people usually make these dishes with a mild or creamy flavor, but my mom came up with a version that’s packed with spice and excitement — perfect for those of us who like a bit of a kick in every bite.

The best part? It’s super easy to make! Just follow the steps carefully, and you’ll end up with a delicious, spicy macaroni that’s comforting, flavorful, and sure to become one of your favorites.

Ingredients (serves 4 people)

  • 1 box Bake Parlour BBQ Macaroni
  • 250 grams boneless chicken (cut into small pieces)
  • 6 tablespoons oil
  • 1 medium onion (chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger (finely chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon garlic (finely chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons cornflour
  • 1 chopped green capsicum
  • 1 carrot (sliced or julienned)
  • 1 small cabbage (finely shredded)
  • 1 small bowl charcoal (for smoke at the end)

Steps

Step 1: Boil the macaroni:

In a small pot, bring water to a boil. Add 1 tbsp of oil and salt to taste. Once the water is boiling, add the macaroni and cook for 7–8 minutes until tender. Drain the water completely, rinse the macaroni with cold water, and then lightly coat it with a little oil to keep it from sticking. Set aside.

Step 2: Prepare the chicken and sauce:

Heat oil in a pan and sauté the onion, ginger, and garlic until light golden brown. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they turn white.

Now, add the Bake Parlour BBQ Macaroni spice sachet (the one that comes in the box). You can also use your own homemade BBQ seasoning or any brand you prefer. Pour in 1 cup of water, cover the pan, and let the chicken cook until it becomes tender.

Step 3: Thicken the sauce and add vegetables:

Once the chicken is cooked, add cornflour to slightly thicken the gravy. Then add the boiled macaroni, chopped capsicum, carrot, and cabbage. Cover and cook for 2 minutes only — the vegetables should stay crisp and colorful, not overcooked.

Step 4: Add the smoky BBQ flavor:

Heat a small piece of charcoal until red-hot. Place it in a small bowl and carefully set it inside the pan. Drizzle a few drops of oil over the charcoal to release smoke, then immediately cover the pan for a few minutes to infuse that rich, smoky BBQ aroma into your macaroni.

Step 5: Serve and enjoy:

Your hot and spicy Chicken BBQ Macaroni is now ready! Enjoy it fresh — full of flavor, spice, and that irresistible smoky touch.
